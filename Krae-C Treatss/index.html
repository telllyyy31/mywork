About Us

Krae-C Treatss was founded by me and my niece started last pandemic 2020.

A little backstory, my mom was diagnosed with ckd5 and diabetes. By that time, I decided to resign at my work to take care of her but then she passed away. Pandemic comes and still i dont have job. I am frustated baker at heart, I have always loved baking and I like sweets.

By April 2020, I started baking with just watching YouTube videos and other tutorials; my favorite was Bigger Bolder Baking by Gemma Stafford. I got highly encouraged to continue to what I've started as a hobby. I started from scratch for basic cupcake flavors. At first, I don't follow the instructions carefully, I did what I think I've learned from the tutorials. Mixing and baking but thenâ€¦ it failed!

After several tryouts and failures, I decided to take baking classes, to learn more and enhance my skills. Took several lessons and recipes at heart and for all we know, we already started in successfully making sweet and savory foods.

Krae-C Treatss is still continuing in serving customers with our foods available currently for delivery and pick-up. We are also looking forward to our store soon wherein our customers can dine in.
